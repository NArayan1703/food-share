Statistics and Impacts

At FoodShare, we are proud of the progress we have made in reducing food waste and making a difference in the lives of individuals and families in need. Here are some key statistics and impacts of our efforts:

Food Donations Made

Since inception, FoodShare has facilitated over 10,000 food donations to various communities.

Meals Served

Through our efforts, we have served more than 100,000 meals to individuals facing food insecurity.

Food Waste Reduction

FoodShare's initiatives have contributed to reducing food waste by approximately 30% in partner establishments.

Community Engagement

Our community of donors and recipients continues to grow, fostering a sense of solidarity and compassion.
